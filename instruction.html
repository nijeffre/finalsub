How to Make Cheesecake

About World History Instructions

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • sugar
  • Sour Cream
  • Vanilla Extract
  • eggs
  • graham Crackers
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Make Filling

Mix together the cream cheese and the sugar until smooth.Add the eggs and vanilla and continue mixing.A mixer could be used if needed. To avoid lumps make sure the cream cheese is cooled to room temperature.

Make Graham Cracker crust

To make the graham cracker crust we have to first finely ground the crackers either using a food processor or a blender. Then mix your crumbs with melted butter, granulated sugar, and a little kosher salt and then press into the bottom of the pan.

Baking

The final step is to place the roasting pan with the cheesecake in the oven to bake for 60-65 minutes.
